What is the Ad Click Forensics?

Ad Click Forensics is our way of presenting things never seen before in your Google Ads stats. With the help of our insight you will be able to cut-off the wasteful traffic. Use this to protect Search, Display, Remarketing, Shopping, and almost any other Google Ads campaign against unwanted and disruptive visitors.

ClickGUARD is collecting 40+ data points that Google Analytics is not, and giving that data to you. Furthermore we offer protection rules that prevent certain patterns of behavior to repeat.

This saves money and keeps the budget only for highly-motivated customers that are likely to convert.
