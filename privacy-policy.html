Privacy Policy

The confidentiality of business data

Preserving the confidentiality of business data is performed in accordance with contractual obligations to previous and current clients (under the NDA contract). The posts on this blog do not contain business data, performance metrics unless it is approved by the client.

I do not disclose confidential information from my clients and previous/future employers in any of the blog posts. If I have an interesting case study, I am always asking for permission to publish the data.

User privacy

I do not share personal information or your email address to third parties. Every site visit is anonymous, and the blog does not use software for collecting personal information from a computer or a mobile device.

When visiting the blog, a cookie will be stored on your computer. Cookie is a small text file that helps web analytics services identify you as a visitor. The web analytics service collects information about your use of the site (the number pageviews, the duration of visit, which operating system you use, your city or country...). More details about Google Cookie Privacy.

You can set your browser to not accept cookies or to remove them after the visit. Also, you can manually remove them yourself at any time. See the manufacturer's documentation to find out specific settings for your browser.

Remarketing and retargeting

Blog uses custom Google Analytics settings that allow retargeting. Retargeting is showing ads on websites outside of this blog, based on your previous visit to the nenad.blog. Third parties or Google display ads to you and other readers of this blog by using the information stored in the cookie.

You can set your browser to not accept cookies or to remove them after the visit. Also, you can manually remove them yourself at any time. See the manufacturer's documentation to find out specific settings for your browser.

I use standard Google Analytics as most sites you visit. Analytics inserts a cookie in your browser's cache. Cookie can be deleted or disabled, if you wish to do so. For all details about browser settings, visit their support websites where you have everything explained.

Your visits are anonymous, and if you contact me by sending me an e-mail or a call, I will not give you or sell your data to third parties.

You can also visit the Google Ads Settings page to enable or disable the display of ads.
